On Saturday night, Claire and I actually got to go out to Break Loose at York's Waterway Raw Bar and Grill. It was a nice venue -- plenty of room in 3 separate areas. I've been out of the scene mostly, so all the music seemed pretty new to me. Since I'm a huge dnb head, and there was none of that here, I wasn't compelled to dance myself to extreme exhaustion.

It looks like Ultraworld has Starscape 2004 coming up in July. Last year's was worthwhile, so maybe I'll try to get to this one as well.
