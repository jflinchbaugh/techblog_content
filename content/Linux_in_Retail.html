I've been surprised over the past couple days to note that I've been seeing Linux machines out in the wild, including Lowes and Pearle Vision. The screens caught my eye, because they were oddly X-looking. Additionally, I saw an XScreensaver locking a terminal at Pearle.

While Pearle's system looked similar, it seems that Lowes is running a system from Ultimate Technology.
