The beer fridge was looking pretty barren last weekend (only 2 Yuengling Lagers), but I fixed it. The nice guy at Kirchner recommended the Harpoon UFO (UnFiltered Offering) Hefeweizen for a good summer wheat beer. It proved to be an excellent recommendation. It's light but tasty and grainy feeling. It goes down like water.

I also picked up a 12-pack of Samuel Smith's Oatmeal Stout. Fortunately, this doesn't go down like water, because that would be awfully expensive.

Finally, Lynn introduced a 6-pack of Brooklyn Brewery's Double Chocolate Stout to round out the collection. It's probably been a year since I've had either the Brooklyn or the Sammy Smith. Good stuff.
