If you ever listen to MarketPlace on your local public radio station, you know it has a different sound with its eclectic mix of music bits between stories.

It seems they now have the playlist on the PM pages next to the stories, and Jane, the music director, has a little weblog about her music picks. Her frequent picks of nice little dance tracks (trip hop, Bjork, etc) always grab me. I also find the little Ani DiFranco clips fun too.
