Claire and I got out to see Clerks II on Saturday. It was pretty funny, but I had a bit of a headache, so that made parts feel a bit slow.

I'm not sure what I was expecting, but it wasn't quite as satisfying as I had hoped. I guess these days, if I'm going to devote some time to something, even a couple hours, it should be teaching me something or changing the way I think about a subject. I'm not sure.

It was definitely better than Jay and Silent Bob Strike Back. Maybe some quotables and scenes will take hold in my brain, and I'll feel the movie was worth the exorbitant theater price.
