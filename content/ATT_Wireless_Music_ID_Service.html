title=AT&T Wireless Music ID Service date=2004-04-15 22:20:58.004 type=post status=published tags=Technology ~~~~~~

I just read an article about AT&T Wireless' new music identification system, so I had to try it out. I dialed "#ID" (#43), held the phone up to my tinny little laptop speaker, and about 30 seconds later it sent me a message correctly identifying Concrete Blonde's Everybody Knows. Awesome!

The only problem is that it costs $0.99 per successful song ID. I'm sure I'll have fun with this when I go clubbing!
