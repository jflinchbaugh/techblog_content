I actually got out to see Micro at Dragonfly in Harrisburg on Friday night. I got in at about 11pm to find a decent turnout and left at about 1am.

I heard mostly Psyche and Micro. The music was great, and I found I could still dance, and quite a lot at that. It's been a long time, since I've felt the energy to just stay out on the dance floor for a majority of the night. It really was great, so I'll have to keep up and get out to the next parties in my area.

All my slacker friends missed a good night. ;)
