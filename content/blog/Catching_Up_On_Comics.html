I have that handy list of comics over in the navbar, but I've not had time to visit in the past month. I've finally gotten back to them save one -- MegaTokyo. It's a bit more involved with more to read and absorb, so I've saved it for last. The artwork is amazing, so it's worth the wait, but I'm starting to think this may be a better one to read in comic book format where I can rip through a bit quicker.
