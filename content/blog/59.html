Comcast has select shows from TechTV available through their on-demand service.

It's amazing how useful and interesting their programming can be. It's not just about gaming. They cover lots of future technology like solar-powered water taxis, critter-cam, and all sorts of various new gadgets. They even had a very impressive Star Trek apartment. I'm pleased, but I still wouldn't mind a full 24-hr channel.
