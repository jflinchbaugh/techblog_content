My New Year's Eve was great. I slept in just a little, then ran around downtown Lancaster with Claire and Paige for lunch and most of the afternoon. We wandered the farmers' market, Hagar Arcade (upscale shops), Queen Street shops, Chestnut Books.

We didn't really buy much beyond lunch, but it was nice to wander. It was actually nice enough that we could just sit on a bench and eat outside. It was nice to get downtown again. I've missed my days being near things working in downtown Lancaster.

Otherwise, we just stayed home for the night. It was nice and relaxing.
