It sounds like The Dixie Chicks are still having a rough time after the anti-Bush comments of one member 3 years ago. They remain mostly unapologetic and bitter about the way the music scene, radio programmers, and fans have treated them as well -- their latest single is titled Not Ready To Make Nice.

I saw them live years and years ago at a Lilith Fair. I don't care much for country music, but we were all howling with laughter and appreciation when they sang the very entertaining Goodbye Earl.

I almost feel like I should go out and buy the new CD just to support them for trying to make their statement.
