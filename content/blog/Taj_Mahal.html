title=Taj Mahal date=2005-03-22 15:37:37.948 type=post status=published tags=Family ~~~~~~

Paige, Claire, and I ran out to Taj Mahal last night for Indian food as my little birthday celebration. It was very nice -- on Mondays, they happen to have a little buffet, so I got to try lots of different dishes. We had no trouble finding good things to eat, which was sort of surprising. I expected to find things I'd prefer to avoid, but that wasn't the case. I could eat anything they had, as could Claire for the most part. Paige wasn't really having any of it, though -- She's sort of picky right now. We'll definitely go back.
