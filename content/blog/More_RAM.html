I extended my notebook's RAM from 512M to 1280M. It's great! No more swapping as JBoss, Gnome, Eclipse, and Firefox take their turns executing, and now I have lots of caching (500M at the momemnt). Life's too short to wait for your applications to swap in and out of memory.
