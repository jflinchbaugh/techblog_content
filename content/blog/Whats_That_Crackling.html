title=What's That Crackling? date=2005-04-11 09:29:32.851 type=post status=published tags=Home, Miscellaneous ~~~~~~

Claire woke up at 2AM or so to a strange little rustling and what-not that seemed to (hopefully) be outside. I went looking around, and glanced out the window to see the neighbor's truck ablaze! I called 911, Millersville Volunteer Fire Company showed up 10 minutes later to put it out, and it didn't blow up, so all is well.
