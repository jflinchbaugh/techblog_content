I watched NBC's American version of The Office last night. It had lots of funny little amusements. The direct and sarcastic receptionist character is probably one of the only highlights.

Its "MTV Real World"-style interview thing is sort of annoying -- too much of a carry over from reality television. It would be funnier if it was just the normal sitcom style. It seems to be Office Space jokes in reality television style.
