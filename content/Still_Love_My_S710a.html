title=Still Love My S710a date=2006-06-22 14:42:22.343 type=post status=published tags=Mobile, SE S710a ~~~~~~

My SonyEricsson S710a is still my favorite phone out there. I thought I'd want the W600 to replace it, but I've seen that phone in person now, and I just don't find it to be as good of a fit for my needs. It has more memory, but doesn't have a removable media card, and the screen is smaller.

The S710a just feels like more of a data device than a music device, which meets my needs. My only remaining complaint with my S710a is the voice quality when the signal is low (in my house, unfortunately).
